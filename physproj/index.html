Physics Emulation in 2-D Platformers

What even is a platformer?

Platformers, specifically 2-D platformers in our case, are video games wherein the player traverses the environment and solves puzzles by jumping on platforms.

What's going on here?

This website aims to provide an understanding of how physics work in 2-D platformers by comparing the tricks used to real life and also giving a thorough breakdown of the code.
